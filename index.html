--- title: Vote No On 303 heading: Vote No On 303 og: title: Vote No On 303 ---

Today, we are disappointed to say that Initiated Ordinance 303 passed, a harmful and cruel ban that will increase policing for our unhoused neighbors and over criminalize poverty. This wasn’t the outcome that we hoped for but we know that our community can still do our best and continue to show up for people experiencing homelessness

The fight isn’t over yet and we remain dedicated to protecting our neighbors. Organizations such as Denver Homeless Out Loud and Colorado Coalition for the Homeless continue to do important work in the community to ensure that we are finding equitable and sustainable solutions to homelessness – this outcome only ignites us to work harder. We hope that you will all continue to show up with us in this fight.
